Key Considerations

  • Collaboration: All teams should maintain open communication channels and regular sync meetings
  • Flexibility: Roles may shift based on project complexity, team expertise, and organizational maturity
  • Scalability: Consider team capacity and skill development when assigning responsibilities
  • Cross-functional: Encourage cross-team knowledge sharing and skill development
  • Continuous Improvement: Regularly review and update this RACI matrix based on lessons learned
