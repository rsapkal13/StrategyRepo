Simplified RACI Matrix - Key Team Responsibilities

  • Group IT Arch CoE: Enterprise technology strategy, architecture governance, security standards, and infrastructure enablement
  • Advanced Analytics: Business alignment, data governance strategy, stakeholder communication, and business intelligence
  • AI Engineering: AI/ML solution architecture, platform operations, model deployment, and incident response
  • Data Engineering: Data infrastructure, pipelines, quality implementation, and system performance
  • Data Science: Model development, statistical analysis, research activities, and AI ethics assessment
  • Clear Separation: IT CoE handles enterprise/platform enablement; Data & AI teams handle solution design and implementation
  • Collaboration Points: Regular architecture reviews, technical consultations, and cross-team coordination meetings
  • Simplified Activities: High-level, abstract categories covering end-to-end capabilities rather than granular tasks
